Differentially Private Survey
January 2019 - April 2019 Edmonton, AB
Differentially Private Survey is a survey application that ensures the confidentiality of the survey responses by deliberately introducing noise into the collected dataset using a differntially private algorithm. This application was built by a team of three for the Capstone Project during my final semester of university.

During the project, I had the opportunity to experience the different software development phases starting from requirements gathering and design to implentation, testing and presentation of the prototype to the public. I am very grateful that I was able to learn a lot from my team members by engaging in interesting discussions about this project and sofware in general.
